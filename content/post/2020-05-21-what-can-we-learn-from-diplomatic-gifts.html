Calculating TF-IDF

So far weâ€™ve been looking at the most common words â€“ the ones that appear in a countryâ€™s descriptions the most times. This measure is called term frequency (the TF in TF-IDF). Since weâ€™re interested in gifts that are distinctive to a specific country, we want to know words that show up a lot in one countryâ€™s descriptions (high term frequency) but donâ€™t show up a lot in other countriesâ€™ descriptions. Letâ€™s use our Moldova example again. TF-IDF quantifies what we demonstrated in the Moldova example: it is a number that describes how â€œdistinctiveâ€ a word is, which turns out to be exactly what weâ€™re interested in.
